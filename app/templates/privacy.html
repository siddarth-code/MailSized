Privacy Policy

Effective date: Aug 18, 2025

What We Collect

  • Uploaded video files (source) and compressed output files for the sole purpose of performing the service.
  • Operational data: file size, format, duration, resolution (used to compute the optimal compression settings).
  • Contact email (required) to deliver the download link.
  • Payment info is processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store card numbers.
  • Basic logs (IP, timestamps, error traces) for security and troubleshooting.

File Retention & Deletion

We operate a privacy-first pipeline:

  • Uploaded source files are processed, then scheduled for deletion.
  • Compressed outputs are kept only to allow download and link delivery via email.
  • Both source and output files are automatically deleted within ~24 hours of completion or link delivery.
  • Expired links become permanently inaccessible.

Security

  • Transport uses HTTPS. Stripe handles all card data on their PCI-compliant platform.
  • Access to processing nodes and storage is restricted and monitored.
  • No watermarks or ad injections into your files.

Cookies & Analytics

  • We may use strictly necessary cookies for session integrity.
  • We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage (no video content is analyzed).
  • Any ad networks you enable (e.g., AdSense) may set their own cookies as governed by their policies.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store card data. For details, see Stripe’s privacy policy.

Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing where applicable by law (e.g., GDPR/CCPA).
  • Contact us to exercise these rights or ask questions at support@mailsized.com.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
