What happens under the hood

Without revealing proprietary heuristics, here’s the high-level process:

  • Probe: We read container/stream metadata (duration, resolution) to estimate an optimal bitrate budget.
  • Targeting: We budget for the provider cap (with safety margin) so the final file reliably attaches.
  • Bitrate split: We reserve audio bits (AAC) and allocate the rest to video intelligently.
  • Scaling: If the bit budget is tight relative to resolution, we downscale to preserve clarity and avoid artifacts.
  • Pass strategy: Long clips or tight budgets trigger a two-pass encode for steadier quality.
  • Fast start: We optimize the MP4 for instant playback and email delivery.

FAQ

Will this work for Gmail, Outlook, and others?
Yes—select the provider and we target that attachment cap with headroom.
How long will compression take?
Depends on file size, duration, and server load. The progress bar updates during processing.
What about very noisy or action-heavy videos?
Complex footage needs more bits. Our pipeline adjusts bitrate and scaling to stay within the email limit while keeping clarity.
When are files deleted?
Automatically after the download window (~30 minutes). See Privacy for details.
