Our Smart, Standards-Based Pipeline

MailSized uses a standards-based compression pipeline trusted by professionals worldwide. We automatically adjust settings based on your file and selected email provider so your video meets real-world attachment limits (e.g., ~25 MB for Gmail, ~20 MB for Outlook) while maintaining clarity. We do not reveal proprietary formulas or exact profiles—so competitors can’t simply copy our work.

  • No guesswork: We target sizes that align with typical provider caps to avoid “message too large” errors.
  • Adaptive by design: Short clips, webcam videos, and well-lit footage compress especially well.
  • Streaming-friendly output: Your final file is prepared for quick start when viewed or attached.

Step-by-Step

  1. Upload: Drag & drop or choose your video. You’ll see upload progress and basic file info.
  2. Select Provider & Extras: Pick Gmail, Outlook, or Other; add optional priority or transcript.
  3. Secure Checkout: We use Stripe for safe payments. You’re redirected back automatically.
  4. Smart Compression: Our engine optimizes your video to meet the chosen email target.
  5. Instant Download: Get a download link on the site and via email. Links expire in ~24 hours.

What Quality Should I Expect?

Real-world expectations (these are practical ranges, not hard promises):

Short clips (≤ 60s):Often very crisp at email-friendly sizes.
Standard videos (1–5 min):Clear and shareable; ideal for email.
Longer content (5–20 min):Readable and watchable; complex motion may soften.
Best results:Good lighting, steady footage, and non-grainy sources.
